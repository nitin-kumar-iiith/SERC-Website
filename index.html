June 16, 2021

Faculty in Software Engineering: The institute invites applicants in all areas of Software Engineering including: Requirements engineering, Software architecture and design, Program analysis, Software testing, Software Metrics, Formal Methods, software processes and paradigms, software quality, etc. Peripheral areas like HCI, AR/VR, Wearable computing will also be considered. Applications will be processed as and when they are received. Candidates with PhD in Computer Science/Software Engineering or a relevant area, an outstanding research record and commitment to teaching are encouraged to apply. The position and level offered would be based on relevant experience in research and teaching. Candidates with ABD status will also be considered. However they are required to complete their PhD before joining IIIT-H. Please apply via http://www.iiit.ac.in/Careers/index.html If you need more information about the position, please contact: Dr. Y. Raghu Reddy (raghu.reddy@iiit.ac.in)
